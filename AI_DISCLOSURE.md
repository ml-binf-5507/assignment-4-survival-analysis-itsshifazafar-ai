## What is Appropriate AI Use? ✅

We encourage you to use AI tools for:
- **Explanations**: "What is censoring?" or "What does a hazard ratio mean?"
- **Debugging**: "Why is my Kaplan–Meier plot failing?" + error message
- **Syntax help**: "How do I fit a Cox model in `lifelines`?"
- **Learning resources**: "Recommend a tutorial on Kaplan–Meier curves or survival analysis"
- **General concepts**: "What is the difference between overall survival and progression-free survival?"

## What is NOT Appropriate? ❌

Do NOT:
- Ask AI to write your entire function for you
- Copy code from AI without understanding it
- Submit code without testing/modifying it
- Hide the fact that you used AI
- Use AI to do the assignment for you
